The Cacti and Scenery of Presidio Co. West Texas

List of Cacti that you can find in Predisio Co. West Texas

  • Opuntia ssp.
  • Ariocarpus fissuratus
  • Epithelantha ssp.
  • Lophophora williamsii

Table of the Cacti in the above list

Species Size Location

Epithelantha ssp. 1-1.5" tall, ~1" wide Partial shade - Full sun; abundant
Lophophora williamsii 0-0.5" tall, 0.25-1.5" wide Flush to ground, Partial shade - Full sun, abundant
